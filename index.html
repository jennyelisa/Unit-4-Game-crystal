Crystal Collector

You will get a random number at th beginning of the game.

There are four crytals below. By clicking on a cyrstal you will add a specifc amount of points to your total score.

You win the game matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score gets above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
